## Graduate School Information Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/7b269dcc505b45e88c1166af189db060/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Feature Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular users, Administrators.

**Regular User Features:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Postgraduate Information
5. Application Guide
6. Material Information
7. Forum Information
8. Personal Center
9. My Posts
10. My Orders
11. My Address
12. My Favorites
13. Shopping Cart
14. Customer Service Chat
15. Change Password
16. My Favorites Management
17. My Orders Management


**Administrator Features:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Postgraduate Information Management
4. Student Management
5. Application Guide Management
6. Material Information Management
7. Material Category Management
8. Forum Management
9. Banner Management
10. Customer Service Management
11. Order Management
